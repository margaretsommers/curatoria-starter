What works in this starter

  1. Well-known returns the free full catalog — design_systems[] with names, prices, and access_url (no storage paths).
  2. GET /catalog is a free alias of the same listing.
  3. Protected asset routes return 402 until payment; then markdown or zip bytes.
  4. Each paid route declares Bazaar discovery metadata automatically in 402 challenges.
  5. Local files under design-systems/ are the simplest source of truth today.
  6. External Bazaar indexing still depends on third-party directories finding your catalog URL.
