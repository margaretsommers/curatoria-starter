Creator guide

Set up your own x402 design catalog.

Follow the starter chapters in docs/creator/ to fork the template, connect a payout wallet, publish products, test x402, and deploy your own catalog.

Start here

Available today: Fork curatoria-starter, connect a wallet, add files under design-systems/, and deploy the Node service.

Coming soon: Connect Google Drive, Dropbox, iCloud, or your domain without moving files; automatic x402 Bazaar registration.

What works in this starter

  1. The public catalog endpoint lists product metadata.
  2. Protected product routes return 402 until payment is supplied.
  3. Local files under design-systems/ are the source of truth today.
  4. Storage connectors and automatic Bazaar registration are planned, not shipped.
